Amity Noida Scholarships 

The University offers up to 100% scholarships to meritorious students as well as to the ones with excellent sports achievements. Below mentioned are the details regarding Amity University scholarships:

Scholarships for UG students:

UG students are awarded a merit-based scholarship based on their HSC scores. This scholarship is offered on a first-come-first-serve basis. Below mentioned are the details regarding the same:

  • 100% Scholarship: A 100% scholarship is awarded to the candidates who have secured 93% aggregate and above (excluding Physical Education/ Fine Arts/ Performing Arts/ Vocational/ non written subjects) in CBSE or ISC Board
  • 50% Scholarship: A 50% scholarship is awarded to the candidates who have secured 90% aggregate and above (excluding Physical Education/ Fine Arts/ Performing Arts/ Vocational/ non written subjects) in CBSE or ISC Board
  • 20% Scholarship: A 20% scholarship is awarded to the candidates who have secured 90% aggregate and above (excluding Physical Education/ Fine Arts/ Performing Arts/ Vocational/ non written subjects) in State Board

Scholarship for PG Students:

A merit-based scholarship is awarded to PG students based on their graduation marks. Students can avail this scholarship on a first-come-first-serve basis. Details regarding the same are as follows:

  • 100% Scholarship: Candidates, who have secured 93% aggregate and above (excluding Physical Education/ Fine Arts/ Performing Arts/ Vocational/ non written subjects) in CBSE/ ISC Board of Class 12 and 80% in graduation, are awarded 100% scholarship.
  • 50% Scholarship: Candidates, who have secured 90% aggregate and above (excluding Physical Education/ Fine Arts/ Performing Arts/ Vocational/ non written subjects) in CBSE/ ISC Board of Class 12 and 75% in graduation, are awarded 50% scholarship.
  • 20% Scholarship: Candidates, who have secured 90% aggregate and above (excluding Physical Education/ Fine Arts/ Performing Arts/ Vocational/ non written subjects) in State Board of Class 12 and 75% and above in graduation, are awarded 20% scholarship.

Sports Scholarship: The University offers a provision of fast-track admission via sports scholarship. Amity offers a 50% scholarship to national players and a 100% scholarship to international players.

Martyrs Scholarship: The University offers up to 100% scholarships to selected students based on the scrutiny of the scholarship committee. Martyrs from any of the following organisations are eligible for this scholarship:

Amity University Noida FAQs

Q. How good is Amity University Noida?

A. Amity University Noida is one of the top private universities. The University is popularly known for its MBA programme. Also, it has a placement record of over 90%.

A. No, candidates need to fill a separate application form for all the courses at Amity University Noida.

Q. Does Amity University Noida conduct any entrance test?

A. Yes, Amity University Noida conducts its own entrance exam, namely Amity JEE for admission to the BTech programme.

Q. When was Amity University Noida established?

A. Amity University Noida was established in 2005 in Sector-125, Noida, Uttar Pradesh by Ritnand Balved Education Foundation.

Q. What is Amity University Noida famous for?

A. Amity University is known for its MBA, BSc, BTech and BBA programmes.

Q. Which courses are offered at Amity University Noida?

A. Amity University Noida offers over 100 courses under several streams, such as Management, Arts, Commerce, Science, Designing, Pharmacy, etc.

Q. Is Amity University Noida approved by UGC?

A. Amity University Noida is approved by the UGC and 11 other statutory bodies.

Q. Can a student change the Amity University Noida campus?

A. Yes, students can change their Amity campus. Candidates need to fill an application form in the second year to change their campus.

Q. How is the faculty at Amity University Noida?

A. Amity University Noida has highly experienced and qualified faculty who have filed over 900 patents and authored over 500 books. The staff benchmarks the latest teaching methodologies to encourage learning. Also, the faculty has published more than 6,000 papers.

Q. Is Amity Noida University's fee refundable?
